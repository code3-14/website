Sierpinski Triangle

What is it?

The Sierpinski triangle is a fractal named after Polish mathematician Waclaw Sierpinski. It consists of an infinite number of repeating triangles which become recursively smaller inside an outermost triangle. It is also called the Sierpinski gasket or the Sierpinski sieve.
